Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a meeting room?

Click on "My Bookings" in the navigation menu, then click "Create New Booking". Select a room, choose your time slot, and submit the form.

How do I cancel a booking?

Go to "My Bookings" and click the "Cancel" button next to the booking you wish to cancel.

What happens if there's a conflict?

The system automatically checks for conflicts. If the room is already booked for your selected time, you will receive an error message and need to choose a different time or room.

How do I view room details?

Click on "Rooms" in the navigation menu to see all available rooms. Click "View Details" next to any room to see its capacity, floor, and current bookings.

Who can I contact for technical support?

Submit a support ticket using the form above, and an administrator will respond as soon as possible.
